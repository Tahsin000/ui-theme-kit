About Sibgat Telecom

Leading mobile phone dealership in Bangladesh since 2010

Our Story

Sibgat Telecom is Bangladesh's premier mobile phone dealership company, with over a decade of experience in the telecommunications industry. We specialize in distributing high-quality mobile phones from Symphony, iTel, and Tecno to retailers and sub-dealers across the country.

With a network of over 500 sales representatives and sub-dealers, we've established a strong presence throughout Bangladesh, ensuring that quality mobile devices reach consumers in every corner of the nation.
