About Me

Mohammad Kafil Uddin is a distinguished Islamic scholar and educator currently serving as the Principal Acting at Baitush Sharaf Akhtaria Adarsh Fazil Degree Madrasa. With a profound commitment to Islamic education and academic excellence, he has dedicated his career to nurturing young minds and promoting the values of Islamic scholarship.

His pedagogical approach combines traditional Islamic teachings with contemporary educational methodologies, creating a balanced learning environment for students. As an administrator, he emphasizes discipline, moral integrity, and academic rigor while fostering an atmosphere of compassion and intellectual curiosity.

Beyond his administrative duties, Mohammad Kafil Uddin is known for his scholarly contributions to Islamic studies and his active participation in educational conferences and seminars across Bangladesh. His vision is to prepare students who excel not only in religious knowledge but also in character and civic responsibility.
